The Wicked Game

A single-player adventure game inspired by Disco Elysium and the vintage comic book art style.

  • Concept
  • Gameplay
  • Art Style

Concept

The Wicked Game is set in the city of Venice, Italy during the annual carnival period. The player takes on the role of Giovanni, a psychotic failed actor who becomes the 22nd player in a deadly game organized by 21 crazy aristocrats.

Gameplay

  • Puzzle-solving: The game features a variety of challenging puzzles that the player must solve in order to progress through the game. These puzzles are based on the game's environment, dialogue, and character interactions, and they require the player to use their wits and their detective skills in order to solve them.
  • Exploration: The game world is open and immersive, with a variety of hidden areas and secrets to discover. The player can explore the city of Venice and interact with its inhabitants in order to gather information and clues that will help them in the game.
  • Dialogue: The game includes a deep and complex dialogue system that allows the player to engage in meaningful conversations with the game's characters. These conversations can influence the game's story and gameplay, and they allow the player to uncover secrets and make decisions that affect the game's outcome.

Art Style

The Wicked Game features a distinctive art style that blends the aesthetics of vintage comic books with modern digital graphics. The game's environments and characters are hand-drawn and carefully detailed, with a vibrant color palette and a stylized, exaggerated look that gives the game a unique and immersive atmosphere.
